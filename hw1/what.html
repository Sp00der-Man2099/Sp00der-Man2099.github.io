The Simulation Machine

A quantum computer could run simulations with impeccable real world accuracy. This could be used in chemistry and medicine to find new solutions and cures. Thanks to entanglement it can solve complex algorithms using techniques not accessible to normal PCs.


There is also a real possibility that someone with a quantum computer could be able to hack into peoples personal data by trying every combination until they get the password exactly. On a normal computer this could take hundreds or even thousands of years to guess a persons password, but on a quantum computer this process could be shortened, creating more problems we will need to think about.
